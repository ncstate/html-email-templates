Oct. 1, 2014

Chancellor's Fall Address

Chancellor Randy Woodson invites you to join him in the ballroom of the Talley Student Union at 10 a.m. on Wednesday, October 1, 2014, for the Chancellor's Annual Fall Address.

In 2010, NC State adopted a 10-year strategic plan that established a vision for increasing student success, investing in faculty and infrastructure, developing research solutions, continuously improving our organization and developing impactful partnerships. At his fall address, Chancellor Woodson will review three years of progress toward those goals and set the course for the future.

Space is limited. Please mark your calendar and plan to arrive early. Light refreshments will be served. We look forward to seeing you on October 1.
